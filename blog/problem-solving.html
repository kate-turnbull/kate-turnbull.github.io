WProblem Solving

Discuss a time you got blocked on a simple problem

While working on the first challenge within the Variables and Objects Kata I got stuck on the second question. I had just been way over thinking the whole exercise. Instead of approaching it step by step I was trying to solve the end problem. I took a step back, and cut it up into little questions, and answered each little question first. I felt much less overwhelmed when cutting it into smaller sections. I learned to approach each error a step at a time, and not let myself get overwhelemed.

Discuss a time you solved a problem in an elegent way

While working on the jumble your words problem in JS-Olympics I had to complete 3 steps to solve the problem, I found a way to solve it by cleanly linking all 3 solutions together. Again I used my method of seperating out the task into smaller steps, and then finessing the code for them to all be appleid within the one function. I felt really happy, that I had figured out how to do soemthing which seemed complex to me. I learned how to attempt to make my code clean.
